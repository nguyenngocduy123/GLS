For example:

/web/data/v2.1/example/:id => http://localhost/web/data/v2.1/example/:id

GET /web/data/v2.1/pod/:jobId

Get proof of delivery of a job

Drivers can only query for proof of delivery of a job that is assigned to his vehicle
