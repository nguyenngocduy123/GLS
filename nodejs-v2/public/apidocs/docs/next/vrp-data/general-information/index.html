Information

All datetime input are in ISO8601 format (YYYY-MM-DDTHH:mm:ssZ)

All time units are in minutes

All weight/capacity units are in kilograms

All costings are in dollars

Special Columns

Status Column

DeliveryDetail table, Status column

ValuePurpose
1Pending
2Late
3On-time
4Unsuccessful
5Expect to be late

Note Columns

DeliveryDetail table, NoteFromDriver and NoteFromPlanner columns

FieldRequiredTypeDefault ValueDescription
keyrequiredStringPurpose of remark (e.g. "Payment mode", "Equipments")
valuerequiredStringRespective comment for the key (e.g. "Cash", "Crane")

VehicleRestriction Column

DeliveryMaster table, VehicleRestriction column

Comma delimited string. May have space between delimiters

PrefixPurpose
(None)Allowed vehicles
#Not allowed vehicles
@Allowed vehicle types
#@Not allowed vehicle types

Vehicle is identified by its Id field

Vehicle type is identified by its Name field

Examples:

  • Truck3Ft (Plate Number SJJ1234M)
    • Vehicle Truck3Ft is allowed to do the order
  • @10Feet
    • Vehicles that are under the category of 10Feet are allowed to do the order
  • #SJJ4567M
    • Vehicle SJJ4567M is not allowed to do the order
  • #@Van
    • Vehicles that are under the category of Van are not allowed to do the order
